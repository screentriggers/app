About This Project

Trigger Library was created to help viewers navigate potentially triggering content in movies and TV shows. By collecting community-submitted notes about specific scenes, we aim to give people more context before they press play.

This project is community-driven: entries are written by volunteers who share what stood out to them. It is not a complete or definitive record of every trigger in a title, and different people may experience the same scene very differently.

Nothing on this site is a substitute for professional mental health care or crisis support. If you are struggling, please reach out to a trusted professional, support line, or community resource in your area.

We hope this library can be one small tool to identify potentially distressing scenes in films and shows to support safer, more informed viewing for everyone.
