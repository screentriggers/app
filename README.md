# 🎬 ScreenTriggers

**Check for potentially distressing content before you press play.**

🔗 **Try it now:**
https://screentriggers.github.io/app/

---

## 🧠 Why this exists

Not every movie is a safe watch—especially if you're navigating grief, trauma, or sensitivity to certain themes.

ScreenTriggers helps you **make informed viewing decisions** by showing user-submitted content warnings for movies—so you can avoid being caught off guard.

---

## 🔍 What you can do

* **Search any movie title**
  Quickly check if a film contains potentially difficult content

* **See real user-submitted triggers**
  Including themes like:

  * grief & loss
  * illness
  * violence
  * and more

* **Contribute to the database**
  Can’t find a movie? Add it in seconds to help someone else

---

## ⚠️ A quick note

All content is **community-submitted**. While we aim to be helpful and accurate, results may vary. Use your judgment and take care of yourself.

---

## ❤️ Who it’s for

* People navigating **grief or loss**
* Anyone sensitive to certain themes in media
* Viewers who want a **heads-up before watching**
